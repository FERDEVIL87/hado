Hado wo Iku Kaze no Gotoshi (2019)
Hado wo Iku Kaze no Gotoshi (覇道を往く風の如し, Hadō o iku kaze no gotoshi, lit. Like The Wind, Going Down The Road) is a character theme for Ultraman Fuma in Ultraman Taiga. It was used in episode 13 during a montage of Fuma's past battles. An instrumental of the song was later used in episode 22.

Artist: Shota Hayama
Released: 2019
Album: Ultraman Taiga Original Soundtrack
